# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

RepoDigest contributors are expected to be respectful, constructive, and inclusive.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, hostile communication, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and review comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates this policy and may restrict participation for repeated violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness violations, contact maintainers privately and include relevant links/screenshots for context.
